--------------------

Huang, Kerson. _Introduction to Statistical Physics_. London, UK: Taylor & Francis, 2001. ISBN: 9780748409419.

Prerequisites

Statistical Physics I ([8.044](/courses/8-044-statistical-physics-i-spring-2013)), Quantum Physics II (8.05)
